Getting Started

  1. Add Income Sources: Navigate to Income & Expenses to record all your income streams and how often you receive them.
  2. Record Expenses: List your fixed recurring expenses (rent, bills) and variable expenses (groceries, gas) to create a realistic budget.
  3. Set Savings Goals: Define what you're saving for and set target amounts to stay motivated and track progress.
  4. Monitor Your Budget: Visit the Budget Overview page to see your total income, expenses, balance, and transaction history.
  5. Review Your Progress: Check Goals & History regularly to see how you're doing against your goals and review past months.
